About PCI

Progress Computer Institute (PCI) is Gonda’s top choice for computer education. We specialize in delivering quality, affordable, and job-oriented computer courses. With a mission to empower every student, we combine practical learning, expert guidance, and the latest curriculum to ensure every learner is industry-ready.


Our Vision

To be a leading institution providing accessible, affordable, and modern computer education in every corner of Gonda and nearby areas, helping youth build a confident future in the digital world.


Our Mission

  • ✔ Offer high-quality, industry-relevant computer education.
  • ✔ Enable hands-on learning through live projects and internships.
  • ✔ Encourage students to think creatively and technically.
  • ✔ Provide support for job placement and career building.
  • ✔ Continuously update course content as per the IT market demand.
